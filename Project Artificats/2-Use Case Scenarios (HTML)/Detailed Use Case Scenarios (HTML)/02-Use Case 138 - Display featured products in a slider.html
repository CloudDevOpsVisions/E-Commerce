138 - Display featured products in a slider

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Vendors have already chosen at least 14 featured products.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
  2. A slider shows with three featured products and two buttons for navigation between next and previous featured products is displayed.
  3. Visitor clicks on the next button.
  4. Another three featured products are displayed.
  5. Repeat step 3 and step 4 for three times.
  6. Visitor clicks on the next button.
  7. Another two featured products and a link to view all featured products is displayed.

Postconditions

None

Alternative Flow

None
