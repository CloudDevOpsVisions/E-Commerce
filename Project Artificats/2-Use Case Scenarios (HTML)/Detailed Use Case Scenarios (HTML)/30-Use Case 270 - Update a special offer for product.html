270 - Update a special offer for product

Primary Actors

  • Vendor

Preconditions

  1. Vendor has been logged in to his account.
  2. Vendor has already added at least on offer.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Vendor navigates to the vendor panel.
  2. Vendor clicks on a specific offer title from list all offers page.
  3. The page shows input fields for offer details which is Offer Title, Image, Product Name, Start and end dates is displayed.
  4. All of the input fields are filled with the current offer details.
  5. The page has a grey (unavailable) update button.
  6. The vendor updates the offer title and end date.
  7. The update button becomes blue (available).
  8. The vendor clicks on the save button.
  9. The offer details are validated.
  10. The update button becomes grey (unavailable) again, indicating that the offer details were updated.

Postconditions

  1. The offer details are updated.
  2. The update is reflected to members and visitors.

Alternative Flow

9.1 The offer title is too long

if the offer title is more than 20 characters length, then

9.1.1. An error message will show indicating that the provided title is too long.

9.1.2 The use case resumes at step 4.
