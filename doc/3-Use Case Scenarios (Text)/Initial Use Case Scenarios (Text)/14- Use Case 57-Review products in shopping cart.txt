PBI Number	: 57

PBI Title	: Review products in shopping cart

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. A product was added to the shopping cart by the visitor.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor hovers on the shopping cart and clicks on the option to view the shopping cart.
	2. The shopping cart page is displayed, showing the added items in rows.
	3. Each row shows a Product information, Total Price and an option to remove it.
	4. The page also has a checkout and continue shopping options.
	5. There is a paging support to navigate between different pages of items.
	6. Visitor clicks on the continue shopping option.
	7. The visitor is returned to the previous page.

	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
