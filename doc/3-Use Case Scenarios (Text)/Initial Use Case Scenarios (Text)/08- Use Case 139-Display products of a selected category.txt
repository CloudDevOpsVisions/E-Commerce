PBI Number	: 139

PBI Title	: Display products of a selected category

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. A category was assigned to an existing product.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page and clicks on a specific sub category.
	2. A page with products under the specified sub category is displayed.
	3. The page shows product from the selected sub category.
	4. There is a paging support to navigate between different pages of products.

	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
