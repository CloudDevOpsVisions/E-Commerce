PBI Number	: 75

PBI Title	: Add selected product to the shopping cart

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. A product was added by a vendor.


Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
	2. Visitor clicks on "Add to cart" option for a specific product.
	3. The product is added to the visitor's shopping cart.
	4. A notification shows that the specified product was added is displayed.

	
Postconditions	:
	1. Product is added to the shopping cart.


Alternative Flow:
	None
