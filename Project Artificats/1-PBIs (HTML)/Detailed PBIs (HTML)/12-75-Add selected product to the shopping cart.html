Title: Add selected product to the shopping cart

Tags: BWF, Public, Vistor

Description:

As a visitor, I want to be able to add a product to my cart with a single click when viewing products so that I can quickly put the product I want to purchase into my cart.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:

  • When click on "Add to cart" for a product, it will be added to the shopping cart.
  • Multiple clicks on "Add to cart" should add same product multiple time in shopping cart.
  • When a product is put into the cart, a popup window indicating that the selected product was added to the shopping cart smoothly appears and after 3 seconds smoothly disappears.
