Id		: 75

Title		: Add product to the shopping cart

Tags		: BWF, Public, Visitor

Description	: 
		As a visitor, I want to add a product to my cart with a single click when viewing 
		products so that I can quickly put the product I want to purchase into my cart.

Acceptance Criteria	:
		
	Action Criteria	:
		* A click on "Add to cart" button for a product should add it to the shopping cart.
		* Multiple clicks on "Add to cart" should increase the quantity of the added product.
		* A popup window indicating that the selected product was added to the shopping cart should
		  smoothly appears and after 3 seconds smoothly disappears when a product is added.
