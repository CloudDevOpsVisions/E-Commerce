PBI Number	: 37

PBI Title	: Access shopping cart from the home page

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. At least one product was added to the shopping cart by the visitor.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
	2. Visitor hovers on the shopping cart icon.
	3. A floating dialog shows a summary of the shopping cart is displayed.
	4. The dialog shows for each product its price, image and link to remove it, also
	   it shows the total price, a link to view the shopping cart in details and a link to checkout.
	5. User hovers out of the dialog to hide it.
	6. The dialog disappears.

	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
