120 - View hot sale products in a slider

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Vendors have already added at least four products.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
  2. A slider shows with one hot sale product and two buttons for navigation between next and previous hot sale products is displayed.
  3. Visitor clicks on the next button.
  4. Another hot sale product is displayed.
  5. Repeat step 3 and step 4 for three times.
  6. A link to view all hot sale products is displayed.

Postconditions

None

Alternative Flow

None
