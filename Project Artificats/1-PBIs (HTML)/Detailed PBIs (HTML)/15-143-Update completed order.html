Title: Update completed order

Tags: BWF, Private, Vendor

Description:

As a vendor, I want an option to update an order in vendor panel so that I can correct any mistake that might exist in any order.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:
  • Quantity of products in order, Price of each product in order, Discount on each product in order, Total price on each product in order, Order Status and Gift wrap option of order should be updatable.
  • First name, Last name, Email, Phone, City, State, Postal code, Country and Address of shipping details should be updatable.
