57 - Review products in shopping cart

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Visitor has already added at least one item into his shopping cart.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor hovers on the shopping cart and clicks on the "View cart" button.
  2. The shopping cart page is displayed, showing the first 20 items in rows.
  3. Each row shows a Product Image, Product Name, Quantity, Total Price and a link to remove it.
  4. The page also has a checkout and continue shopping buttons.
  5. There is a paging area where visitor can navigate between different pages which displays the rest of the items.
  6. Visitor clicks on the continue shopping button.
  7. The visitor is returned to the previous page.

Postconditions

  1. None

Alternative Flow

None
