Order		: 10

Id		: 37

Title		: Access the shopping cart from the home page

Tags		: BWF, Public, Visitor

Description	: 
		As a visitor, I want to see icon/button of the shopping cart on the home page, so that I can quickly access my 
		shopping cart. Also, I want to see product count of added products and total price on shopping cart icon/button 
		so that I can know how many product has been added to shopping cart and how much money I’m going to pay.

Acceptance Criteria	:
		
	Action Criteria	:	
		* Display shopping cart icon/button with number of items in the cart and total price of products.
		* On click this icon, display a floating dialog that display a summary of shopping cart, includes:
			+ Number of products along with small product image.
			+ Total price of all products.
			+ Button/link to remove a listed product from shopping cart.
			+ Button/link to view detailed view/page of shopping cart.
			+ Button/link to checkout.
