28 - View top new arrival products in a slider

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Vendors have already added at least 14 products.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
  2. A slider shows with three new arrival products and two buttons for navigation between next and previous new arrival products is displayed.
  3. Visitor clicks on the next button.
  4. Another three new arrival products are displayed.
  5. Repeat step 3 and step 4 for three times.
  6. Visitor clicks on the next button.
  7. Another two new arrival products and a link to view all new arrival products is displayed.

Postconditions

None

Alternative Flow

None
