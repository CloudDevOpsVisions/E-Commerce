PBI Number	: 120

PBI Title	: Display top hot sale products in a slider

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. Vendors have already added some products.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
	2. A slider with a hot sale product and navigation support between hot sale products
	   is displayed.
	3. Visitor navigates to the next hot sale product.
	4. Another hot sale product is displayed.
	5. Repeat step 3 and step 4 for three times.
	6. A link to view all hot sale products is displayed.

	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
