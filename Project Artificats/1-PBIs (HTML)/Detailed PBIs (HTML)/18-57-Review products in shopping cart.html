Title: Review products in shopping cart

Tags: BWF, Public, Vistor

Description:

As a visitor, I want to review the details of the content for the shopping cart at anytime so that I can correct any mistake.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:

  • Continue shopping button should be included.
