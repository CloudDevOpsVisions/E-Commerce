37 - Access the shopping cart from the home page

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Visitor has already added one product or more to his shopping cart.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
  2. Visitor hovers on the shopping cart icon.
  3. A floating dialog shows a summary of the shopping cart is displayed.
  4. The dialog shows for each product its price, image and link to remove it, also it shows the total price, a link to view the shopping cart in details and a link to checkout.
  5. User hovers out of the dialog to hide it.
  6. The dialog disappears.

Postconditions

None

Alternative Flow

None
