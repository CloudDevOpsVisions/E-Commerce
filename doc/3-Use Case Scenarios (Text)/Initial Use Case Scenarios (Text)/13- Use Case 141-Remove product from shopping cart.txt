PBI Number	: 141

PBI Title	: Remove product from shopping cart

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. A product was added to the shopping cart by the visitor.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page and hovers on his shopping cart.
	2. A product is displayed in the shopping cart.
	2. Visitor clicks on the remove icon next to the specified product.
	3. The product is removed from the shopping cart.

	
Postconditions	:
	1. The Shopping cart is updated.


Alternative Flow:
	None
