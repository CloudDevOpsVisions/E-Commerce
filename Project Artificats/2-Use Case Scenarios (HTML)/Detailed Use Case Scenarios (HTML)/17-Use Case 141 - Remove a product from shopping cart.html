141 - Remove a product from shopping cart

Primary Actors

  • Visitor

Preconditions

  1. Visitor has already added three items of a specific product to his shopping cart.

Basic Flow (Happy Path)

  1. Visitor navigates to the home page and hovers on his shopping cart.
  2. Visitor clicks on the remove icon next to the specified product.
  3. One item of the product is removed, 2 remaining.
  4. The number of items in the shopping cart is updated to be 2.
  5. Visitor clicks again on the remove icon for the same product.
  6. One more item of the product is removed, 1 remaining.
  7. The number of items in the shopping cart is updated to be 1.

Postconditions

  1. The shopping cart is updated with the new quantity and total price.

Alternative Flow

None
