PBI Number	: 141

PBI Title	: Remove product from shopping cart

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. At least one item was added to the shopping cart by the visitor.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page and hovers on his shopping cart.
	2. A product is displayed in the shopping cart with one item of it.
	2. Visitor clicks on the remove icon next to the specified product.
	3. The product is removed from the shopping cart.

	
Postconditions	:
	1. The shopping cart total price and quantity are updated.


Alternative Flow:
	None
