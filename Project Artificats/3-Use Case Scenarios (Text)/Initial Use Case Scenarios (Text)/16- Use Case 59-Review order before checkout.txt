PBI Number	: 59

PBI Title	: Review order before checkout

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. A product was added to the shopping cart by the visitor.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to his shopping cart, confirms his information and clicks on review order option.
	2. The order review page shows the items ordered in rows and shopping cart information is displayed.
	3. Each row shows a Product information and controls.
	4. The visitor's personal information is also displayed.
	5. The page shows an option to edit the address and other personal information, an option to 
	   continue shopping and an option to continue the checkout.


	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
