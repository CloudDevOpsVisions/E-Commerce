Title:Display special offer products in a slider

Tags: BWF, Public, Vistor

Description:

As a visitor, I want to see special offers for products in a slider that exist on the home page above featured product's slider and on the right side to categories so that I can know about special offers.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:
  • Three products in a slider should be displayed.
  • Buttons for moving between all three products in slider should be included.
  • All three products should be loaded at once.
  • On click on any product from slider, detailed view for that product should be displayed.
