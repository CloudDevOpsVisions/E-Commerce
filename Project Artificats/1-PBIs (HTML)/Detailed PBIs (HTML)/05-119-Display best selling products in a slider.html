Title: Display best selling products in a slider

Tags: BWF, Public, Vistor

Description:

As a visitor, I want to see best selling products in a slider that exist on the home page so that I can know about best selling products.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:
  • One product at a time in slider's page should be displayed.
  • Two buttons for moving to next one and previous one product in slider should be included.
  • One products should be loaded in advance from current one (next to current page and previous to current page).
  • On click on any product from slider, detailed view for that product should be displayed.
  • Limit number of pages in slider to be maximum 5 (it could be only one page if database does not have enough featured products), last item of 5th page should be a link titled "View All".
  • Last item of last page of slider ("View All") should open all best selling products on a separate view/page where products will be displayed in descending order of their number of sale/order.
