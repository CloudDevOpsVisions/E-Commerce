Id		: 141

Title		: Remove product from shopping cart

Tags		: BWF, Public, Visitor

Description	: 
		As a visitor, I want to remove a product from shopping cart by a button/link so that 
		I can remove unwanted products from my shopping cart.
		
Acceptance Criteria	:
		
	Action Criteria	:	
		* A Remove button should be displayed for each product in shopping cart.
		* A Product should be removed from shopping cart by clicking on remove button.
		* Shopping cart price, quantity and total should be updated when a product is removed 
		  from the shopping cart.
