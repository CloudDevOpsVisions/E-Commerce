Title: Remove a product from shopping cart

Tags: BWF, Public, Vistor

Description:

As a visitor, I want an option to remove a product from shopping cart by a button/link so that I can remove unwanted products from shopping cart.

Acceptance Criteria:

Action Criteria:
  • Remove button should be present with each product in shopping cart.
  • Product should be removed from shopping cart by clicking on remove button.
  • When removing a product from shopping cart, it should be updated for price, quantity and total.
