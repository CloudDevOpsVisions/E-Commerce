PBI Number	: 119

PBI Title	: Display top best selling products in a slider

Primary Actors	:
	1. Visitor

Preconditions	:
	1. Vendors have already added at least four products.

Basic Flow (Happy Path):
	1. Visitor navigates to the home page.
	2. A slider with one best selling product and buttons for navigation between 
	   next and previous best selling products is displayed.
	3. Visitor clicks on the next button.
	4. Another best selling product is displayed.
	5. Repeat step 3 and step 4 for three times.
	6. A link to view all best selling products is displayed.

	
Postconditions	:
	None

Alternative Flow:
	None
